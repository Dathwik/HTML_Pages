Step-by-Step Guide for Guests

1

Get Your Ticket

You'll receive your guest ticket from me electronically. Please print it out before the day of the ceremony — digital/mobile tickets are not accepted at the door. Arriving without a printed ticket will cause significant delays getting in.

2

Arrive Early

Doors open at 9:00 AM — one full hour before the ceremony. Plan to arrive right at 9:00 AM to allow time for parking, security screening, and finding a good seat. Note that it's a weekday, so expect heavier-than-normal traffic around the NJIT campus.

3

Getting There

Full driving and public transit directions to NJIT are available at:

  • njit.edu/about/visit
4

Parking (Free!)

Free parking is available at two NJIT parking decks. Carpooling is strongly encouraged due to multiple ceremonies happening on the same day.

  • Science & Technology Park Garage — 42 Wilsey Street, Newark, NJ
  • Parking Deck — 154 Summit Street, Newark, NJ
📌 View the campus parking map at njit.edu/parking/campus-parking-map-0
5

Bag Policy — Travel Light

To get through security quickly, please follow the bag rules strictly:

  • Only soft-sided clutches or purses under 4" × 7" are allowed
  • No backpacks of any kind
  • Medical or childcare bags under 12" × 16" × 6" are permitted but will be inspected
⚠️ Bags that don't comply must be returned to your vehicle before entry.
6

Seating

All guest seating is general admission — first come, first served. No reserving or blocking seats is permitted. Arriving early (Step 2!) is your best bet for a great spot.

7

Can't Make It? Watch the Live Stream

The ceremony will be live streamed at njit.edu — so you can still celebrate along from anywhere!

Prohibited Items — Do Not Bring

Guests found with prohibited items will be denied entry or ejected from the venue.

  • Outside food or drinks
  • Strollers
  • Wrapped gifts or packages
  • Balloons, beach balls, frisbees
  • Selfie sticks or GoPros
  • Air horns, whistles, vuvuzelas
  • Signs larger than 8½" × 11"
  • Backpacks or large bags
  • Alcohol or illegal substances
  • Aerosol cans or sprays
  • Drones or unmanned aircraft
  • Weapons of any kind
  • Laser pens or pointers
  • Professional camera equipment
  • Skateboards or hoverboards
  • Animals or pets (except service animals)
♿ Accessibility Needs?
Accessible parking is available in Lots 5 and 10. Wheelchair assistance is available inside the BWEC on a first-come, first-served basis — please bring your own wheelchair for use outside the building. Drop-off is available curbside at 100 Lock Street.

For sign language interpreter requests or any other assistance, contact NJIT at commencement@njit.edu
