🔑 System Rules

🏋️
5 PM Gym is Sacred
Nothing gets scheduled in the 5–8:30 PM slot. Interviews, calls, errands happen before or after — never during.
🍽️
Eat Before 4 PM
Pre-workout meal must be done by 4 PM. Don't train on empty or right after a heavy meal.
💪
Protein First, Always
200g protein is the daily anchor. Even on cheat days, prioritize protein in every meal.
🚆
Commute = Learning Time
~2 hrs of free learning daily. Podcasts, audiobooks, flashcards. No social media on the train.
📊
Track Every Application
Spreadsheet: Company, Role, Date Applied, Status, Follow-up. You can't manage what you can't measure.
✍️
Sunday = Weekly Reset
Weekly review every Sunday: applications sent, lifts progressed, lessons learned. Plan the next week consciously.
