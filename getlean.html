Key Rules

  • Hit 150g protein every single day — this is non-negotiable for preserving muscle on a cut
  • Progressive overload — add 2.5–5 lbs to main lifts every week
  • HIIT after Push only — legs get steady/low-impact cardio to avoid overtraining
  • Drink 3L of water daily — especially with the high fruit carb load
  • Don't skip rest day — at 6x/week intensity, Sunday recovery is part of the plan
  • Weigh in every Sunday morning — fasted, same time each week
  • Kombucha post-snack — gut reset and light electrolyte replenishment 🫧
